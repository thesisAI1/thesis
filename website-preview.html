HOW IT WORKS
  1. 1 Post your thesis on X, tag the agent, and paste the token contract address.
  2. 2 The Faculty reviews the author, the token, and the thesis — graded A–F, live.
  3. 3 An A or B grade gets funded: the agent buys on Base with take-profit / stop-loss.
  4. 4 On a winning exit the profit splits four ways — and the agent replies on your post to send your 25%.

GETTING PAID

Your 25% — paid on X

There is nothing to sign up for and no wallet to connect here. When one of your theses closes in profit, the agent comes to you — right under your original post.

1 The agent replies to your thesis

When the trade exits in profit, the committee posts a reply telling you exactly what your 25% author share is — and asks for a Base wallet address.

2 You reply with your wallet

Reply to that tweet with a 0x address. Only the account that posted the original thesis can claim the payout — a reply from anyone else is ignored, so it cannot be hijacked.

✓ The payout is sent on-chain

The agent sends your ETH on Base and replies with the transaction link. Your wallet is remembered, so any future win pays you straight away.
